Product Details

The Adventure Pro Backpack is designed for explorers who demand both durability and functionality. Constructed with high-density nylon and waterproof coating, this backpack will withstand whatever adventures you throw at it.

Features

  • 30L capacity with expandable storage up to 35L
  • Padded back panel and shoulder straps for comfort
  • Water-resistant zippers and sealed seams
  • Multiple access points including top-load, front panel, and side access
  • Hidden anti-theft pocket for valuables
  • Integrated rain cover stores in dedicated bottom pocket
  • Hydration sleeve compatible with 3L reservoirs

Specifications

Material 420D Ripstop Nylon
Dimensions 20" H x 13" W x 8" D
Weight 2.4 lbs
Laptop Compartment Fits up to 15" laptop
